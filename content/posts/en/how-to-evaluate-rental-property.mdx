## Practical Tips

<Callout type="warning">
**Remember**: Always plan for the pessimistic scenario. What happens if the property sits vacant for 3 months?
</Callout>

1. **Check the history** - Who lived there before? Why did they leave?
2. **Know the tenants** - Good tenants are worth more than high yield
3. **Think about exit** - Will you be able to sell the property easily?
